Outdoor Grill Instructions

Before You Start:

  • The grill is located on the back patio
  • Propane tank is connected and ready to use
  • Grilling tools are in the drawer of the grill cart

How to Use:

  • Step 1: Open the grill lid completely
  • Step 2: Turn on the propane tank valve (turn counter-clockwise)
  • Step 3: Turn the left burner knob to "High" and press the ignition button
  • Step 4: Once lit, turn on additional burners as needed
  • Step 5: Let the grill preheat for 10-15 minutes
  • Step 6: After cooking, turn all knobs to "Off"
  • Step 7: Turn off the propane tank valve (turn clockwise)
‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Never light the grill with the lid closed! Always check that burners are off before turning on the propane tank.

After Use:

  • Use the wire brush (in the grill cart) to clean the grates while still warm
  • Ensure all burners and the propane tank are turned off
  • Close the grill lid
  • Cover with the grill cover (hanging on the side of the deck)
üí° Tip: If the igniter doesn't work, you can use a long lighter. Check the propane level gauge on the tank - if it's low, please let us know.

Washing Machine:

  • Add clothes (don't overload - fill no more than 3/4 full)
