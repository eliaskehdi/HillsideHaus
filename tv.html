Quick Start

Remotes Location: On the coffee table or in the drawer beneath the TV

Main Remote: Black Samsung remote controls TV and volume

Streaming: Roku remote (purple buttons) for Netflix, Hulu, etc.

Streaming Services Available

  • Netflix - Already logged in
  • Disney+ - Already logged in
  • Hulu - Already logged in
  • Amazon Prime Video - Already logged in
  • YouTube - Feel free to use
Note: Please don't change any account settings or passwords. You're welcome to create your own profile on services that allow it.

How to Watch TV

  1. Press the power button on the Samsung remote (TV will turn on)
  2. The Roku should turn on automatically
  3. Use the Roku remote to navigate to your desired streaming app
  4. Use the Samsung remote to adjust volume

Sound System

  • The soundbar turns on automatically with the TV
  • Adjust volume using the Samsung TV remote
  • For better sound, make sure the soundbar power light is on

Troubleshooting

  • No picture? Check that TV is on correct input (HDMI 1)
  • No sound? Check soundbar is powered on and volume isn't muted
  • Roku not responding? Try replacing batteries (spares in kitchen drawer)
  • App not working? Exit the app and restart it
