Before You Start

  • Type: Propane gas grill
  • Propane Tank: Located in the storage box under the grill
  • Grilling Tools: In the third drawer on the left side of the kitchen island

How to Use the Grill

  1. Remove the weather cover
  2. Check that the propane tank is connected and has gas
  3. Open the grill lid completely
  4. Turn the propane tank valve to "OPEN" (turn counterclockwise)
  5. Turn the left burner knob to "HIGH" while pressing the ignition button
  6. Once lit, turn on additional burners as needed
  7. Close the lid and preheat for 10-15 minutes
  8. Clean the grates with the wire brush (in the tool drawer)
  9. Place food on the grill and cook as desired
  10. Monitor your food - never leave the grill unattended

Shutting Down the Grill

  1. Turn all burner knobs to "OFF"
  2. Turn the propane tank valve to "CLOSED" (turn clockwise)
  3. Let the grill cool for at least 30 minutes
  4. Once cool, brush the grates clean
  5. Close the lid
  6. Turn the propane tank valve to "CLOSED" (turn clockwise)
  7. Cover the grill with the weather cover

Troubleshooting

  • Grill won't light? Check propane tank is open and has gas
  • Weak flame? Check for blockages in burner tubes (spiders love these!)
  • Uneven heating? Clean the burner ports with a wire brush
  • Out of propane? Exchange the tank for a new one at Ace Hardware located at 59 Church St

Cleaning After Use

  • Brush grates while still warm (easier to clean)
  • Grill tools are dishwasher safe
