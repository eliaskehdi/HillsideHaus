  • Most plates, bowls, and cups (ceramic, glass, and plastic)

‚ùå NOT Dishwasher Safe Items

  • Cast iron cookware - Hand wash only
  • Non-stick pans - Hand wash to preserve coating
  • Wooden utensils and cutting boards - Will crack and warp
  • Sharp kitchen knives - Dulls the blades
  • Fine china or antique dishes - Located in dining room cabinet
  • Copper or brass items - Will tarnish
  • Insulated mugs and water bottles - Can damage insulation
  • Anything with gold or silver trim

How to Use the Dishwasher

  1. Scrape off large food particles (no need to rinse)
  2. Load dishes with dirty side facing the center spray arm
  3. Place cups and bowls on the top rack at an angle
  4. Put plates and larger items on the bottom rack
  5. Place utensils in the basket with handles down (knives up for safety)
  6. Add detergent pod to the dispenser (pods are under the sink)
  7. Close the door firmly until it clicks
  8. Press the "Normal" cycle button
  9. Press "Start"

Troubleshooting

  • Won't start? Make sure the door is fully closed and latched
  • Dishes not clean? Ensure spray arms can rotate freely
  • Water on floor? Check that the door seal is clean and undamaged
